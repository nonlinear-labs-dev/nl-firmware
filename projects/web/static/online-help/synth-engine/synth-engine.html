5. The Synthesis Engine

The C15’s first synthesis engine is named „Phase 22“. It can be seen as the latest stage of an ongoing, long-time development, combining and advancing experiences gained from precursor projects like Spark, Prism, Skanner and Kontour (available for Native Instruments’ acclaimed Reaktor series).
There has been a steady evolution and optimization of the overall architecture and signal flow, as well as the modulation mechanism and feedback structures. This process will continue, further refining and advancing the synthesis engine.
