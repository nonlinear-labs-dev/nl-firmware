Headphones Connector and Volume

back to Overview

The 6.3 mm stereo headphone socket is suitable for all kinds of headphones but we strongly recommend to turn down the level when connecting low-impendance ear plugs.
The output level is independent from the main output level (adjustable by the right hand potentiometer).
